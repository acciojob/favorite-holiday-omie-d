Paris, France


Paris, the city of lights, is famous for its rich history, iconic landmarks, and vibrant culture. It's a must-visit destination for art, food, and architecture lovers.

Top 5 Attractions:

  1. Eiffel Tower – Iconic symbol of Paris offering panoramic city views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to thousands of masterpieces including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – Stunning Gothic architecture in the heart of the city.
  4. Sacré-Cœur – Basilica on Montmartre hill with amazing city vistas.
  5. Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue for shopping and entertainment.

Must-Try Foods:

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargots – Traditional French snails cooked in garlic butter.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate almond-based sweets.
  • Coq au Vin – Chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
